MTM Staff

Meet Our Team

John

John is the coFounder of MTM Studio. John is our sound engineer. He produces quality work and is responsible for assembling, operating and maintaining the technical equipment used to record, amplify, enhance, mix or reproduce sound.

Timothy

Timothy is the coFounder of MTM Studio. He is our music producer and works closely with John. Tim has been playing piano from his young years and chose to pursue a career in the music industry. Both Tim and John spend time together improvising and playing music together.

Emily

Emily works as our hospitality organizer. She handles and makes our clients welcomed. Emily supervises and coordinate various tasks like managing our housekeeping team, food and beverage, and other routine operations.

Jessica

Jessica has joined our team in 2018, taking the responsibility as our Booking/Music manager. She works closely with artists, handling tasks such as 'shopping' demo tapes, booking room spaces, overseeing marketing efforts, handling studio time finances, and managing all other aspects of the business side of music performance.
