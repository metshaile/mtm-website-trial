About us

MTM was first established in 2017. Our creators, John and Timothy, envisioned a space where artists and musicians can come and get their projects done. Both graduating from the top tier school of Music, they both choose to give back their time to the upcoming artists. MTM’s vision is “More Than Music”. We believe that every talent starts from somewhere and grows into something great.
